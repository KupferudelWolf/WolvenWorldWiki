**Myla** is one of the four [[Ætherwolf|ætherwolves]]. She is [[Avia|Avia's]] sister.

## Appearance
Myla is a white wolf with round cyan markings on her pelt. Her eyes are also cyan, as are the illusionary "wings" on her back.

## Personality
Myla is absentminded and prone to dissociating. She is incredibly shy and tends to be quiet, talking mostly in short bursts with occasionally strange wording. She prefers to be alone and unseen, to the point that she has learned to make herself nearly invisible.
