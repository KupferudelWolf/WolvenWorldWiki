## Notes
- C, M, Y, K
- W is white (wildcard); can be anything
- Spells must be learned.
    - Level must be equal to or greater than the number of flux in the cost.
- System implications:
    - Spells that use two perpendicular colors are easier to counter since two colors can reduce its effect.
    - Spells that use two opposing colors are harder to counter but can cause flux to disappear easier.

## Affinity 0
These use a negligible amount of flux and can be casted by anyone familiar with the spell.

> [!cards|2 flex]
> 
> > [!infobox|bg-c-white]
> > ###### Control Drone
> > | | |
> > | ---- | :--- |
> > | *Target* | number of drones you can sense equal to your level |
> > ###### 
> > | |
> > | :-- |
> > | You channel a weak but steady stream of flux to control a drone you are attuned to and can sense. You may command your drone to perform any of its pre-programmed actions. |
